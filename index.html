About Us

At Youth Data Science Initiative, we believe that empowering young minds with data literacy can transform their futures—and the world. Our nonprofit helps middle school and early high school students explore the power of data through hands-on experiences, mentorship, and community collaboration.

Our mission is not just to teach coding and data analysis skills, but to inspire curiosity, resilience, and creativity.

We aim to build a community of young thinkers who understand how data shapes the world—from healthcare to sustainability. By fostering early exposure to these tools, we hope to bridge equity gaps in STEM education and create a more inclusive future.
